Best Everfit Alternative in 2026: A Detailed Feature Comparison

February 20, 2026 9 min read

Everfit has earned a loyal following since launching in 2019. It has a clean interface, strong AI features, and solid workout programming tools. For trainers who primarily need to build and deliver workout programs, it does the job well.

But if you have been using Everfit for a while, you have probably noticed some gaps. No built-in scheduling. No real marketplace. Limited coaching structure beyond workout delivery. These are not minor inconveniences — they are missing pieces of the business puzzle.

If you are looking for a platform that goes beyond workout delivery and helps you run an entire coaching business, here is why trainers are switching to FirstRep.

The 3 Biggest Gaps in Everfit

1. No Built-In Scheduling

This is Everfit's most significant missing feature. There is no way to set your availability, accept bookings, manage appointments, or sync with your calendar — all within the platform.

If you coach in-person or offer video sessions, you need a separate scheduling tool (Calendly, Acuity, Google Calendar) and then manually coordinate between platforms. That means clients are not booking directly through your coaching app, payment is disconnected from scheduling, and you are managing yet another tool.

FirstRep has a complete scheduling system built in: weekly availability, exception dates, buffer time between sessions, appointment types, client booking flow, automatic calendar sync with Google Calendar, and even Google Meet link generation for virtual sessions.

2. No Real Client Marketplace

Everfit has a marketplace feature, but it is in beta and limited to select countries. For most trainers, it might as well not exist. There is no GPS-based discovery, limited filtering, and no active client acquisition engine.

FirstRep's marketplace is a fully functional discovery platform. Clients search by specialty, location, price range, rating, and availability. Trainers can showcase promo videos, before/after transformations, and verified specialty badges. It works the way clients expect to find services in 2026 — like searching for a restaurant or a therapist.

3. Shallow Coaching Structure

Everfit excels at workout delivery. Its workout builder is strong, AMRAP/EMOM support is excellent, and the AI features are solid. But coaching is more than just sending workouts.

What happens after a client subscribes? How do you onboard them? How do you track whether they have actually started their program? How do you know which of your 30 clients needs attention right now?

Everfit leaves most of this to the trainer to figure out manually. FirstRep has built-in systems for every stage of the coaching journey.
