Best Trainerize Alternative in 2026: Why Trainers Are Switching to FirstRep

February 20, 2026 10 min read

Trainerize has been the go-to platform for online personal trainers since 2012. With 400,000+ fitness professionals on the platform, it has the brand recognition, the integrations ecosystem, and the track record. So why are independent trainers starting to look elsewhere?

The short answer: Trainerize was built for a different era of personal training. It excels at workout delivery and has solid integrations. But it was not designed to solve the two biggest problems facing independent trainers today: finding clients and preventing burnout while scaling.

Here is exactly why thousands of trainers are making the switch to FirstRep — and what they wish they had known sooner.

The Real Problems With Trainerize (That Nobody Talks About)

1. Pricing That Punishes Growth

Trainerize's pricing is structured in tiers that get expensive fast. The free tier allows just 1 client with limited features. Want to coach 5 clients? That is $60/month. Need 50 clients? $100/month. And many features are gated behind higher tiers.

Here is how the pricing actually compares:

25 clients $100/mo (Studio tier) $44.50/mo
50 clients $100/mo (Studio tier) $82/mo
75 clients $250+/mo (Enterprise) $119.50/mo
Feature gating? Yes — nutrition, automations, branding locked to higher tiers No — every feature on every plan

The key difference: FirstRep gives you every feature from day one. There is no moment where you outgrow your plan and suddenly need to pay $40 more per month to unlock automations or nutrition tracking.

2. No Built-In Client Acquisition

Trainerize has trainerize.me, a directory where trainers can list their profiles. But it is a passive listing — there is no active discovery engine, no GPS-based search, no filtering by price range or availability. Trainers still need to drive all their own traffic.

This matters because client acquisition is the number one pain point for independent trainers. You can have the best workout programming tool in the world, but if you cannot find clients, it does not matter.

FirstRep has a built-in marketplace where clients actively search for trainers by specialty, location, price, and rating. Trainers get discovered the way people discover restaurants on Google Maps — not through a static directory page.

3. Commission on Every Transaction

When clients pay through Trainerize, the platform takes a cut via payment processing fees on every transaction. There is no distinction between clients you found yourself and clients the platform found for you.

FirstRep takes a different approach: 0% commission on clients you bring yourself. Commission (20%) only applies to clients who discover you through the FirstRep marketplace. Your existing clients? Your revenue stays yours.

4. Web-First, Mobile-Second

Trainerize was built as a web platform first, with mobile apps added later. This is a commonly reported frustration — the mobile experience feels like a web app squeezed into a phone. Program building on mobile is tedious. Navigation feels clunky.

FirstRep was built mobile-first from the ground up. Every screen, every interaction was designed for how trainers actually work — on their phone, between sessions, on the gym floor.
