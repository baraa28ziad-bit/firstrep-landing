Core Platform

Six Pillars of a Better Coaching Business

Each feature is designed to save you time, delight your clients, and grow your revenue.

AI Workout Builder

Generate personalized programs in seconds from a client questionnaire. AI picks exercises, sets, reps, and progression rules from a library of 1,734 exercises. You review, tweak, and assign.

Coaching Dashboard

Health scores, risk signals, check-in queues, and compliance tracking all in one view. See every client's status at a glance and know exactly who needs attention today.

Nutrition & Habits

Auto-calculated macros, custom meal plans, food logging, and daily habit tracking. Your clients build consistency while you monitor compliance without the manual overhead.

Marketplace

Get discovered by thousands of clients searching for their next coach. Your profile, packages, reviews, and specialties are all searchable in a public trainer marketplace.

Smart Accountability

Automated missed-workout nudges, streak tracking, milestone celebrations, and churn prediction. The system catches at-risk clients before they disengage, so you can intervene early.

Integrated Payments

Stripe-powered subscriptions, session packs, and one-time purchases built right in. Clients pay seamlessly, you get paid automatically, and the platform handles invoicing and payouts.
