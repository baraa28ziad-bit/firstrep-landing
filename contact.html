FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is FirstRep really free?
Yes, FirstRep is completely free for trainers with up to 3 clients. When you're ready to grow, the Pro plan starts at just $19/month and scales with your business. No credit card required to get started.
How do I get paid as a trainer?
Stripe handles all payment processing securely. When clients subscribe to your coaching packages, the money goes directly to your bank account via Stripe Express. You get a full revenue dashboard to track earnings, payouts, and subscription metrics.
Can I import my existing clients?
Absolutely. You can invite existing clients via email, a shareable link, or a QR code. Clients you invite directly are considered external clients, so there's no marketplace commission on their subscriptions.
What if I need help getting started?
Our support team is here to help. Email support@firstrep.app for any questions, or use the in-app chat for real-time assistance. We also have a comprehensive help center with guides, tutorials, and video walkthroughs.
