The Complete Progress Picture

Every metric that matters, tracked automatically.

Body Measurements

Track weight, body fat, chest, waist, hips, arms, and custom measurements. Trend charts show progress over weeks and months.

Progress Photos

Secure, private photo storage with side-by-side comparisons. Front, side, and back views. Date-stamped for visual proof of transformation.

Personal Records

Auto-detected PRs after every workout. 1RM estimates, volume records, and exercise-specific bests. Celebrated with push notifications.

Training Volume

Weekly and monthly volume tracking by muscle group. See if clients are progressing, maintaining, or declining. Compare across time periods.

Monthly Reports

Auto-generated comprehensive reports: training stats, nutrition compliance, habit scores, measurement changes, and photo comparisons. Zero manual work.

Wearable Sync

Connect Apple Health and Google Fit for step counts, heart rate, sleep data, and active calories. A complete picture of client wellness beyond the gym.
