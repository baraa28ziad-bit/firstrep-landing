Communication Tools

Every Channel, One Platform

Professional communication tools built for coaches, not just chat apps.

Direct Messaging

1-on-1 messaging with text, images, and links. Message history persists across sessions. Clients can reach you, you reply on your schedule.

Voice Notes

Record and send voice messages for personalized feedback. Faster than typing, more personal than text. Clients hear your voice without a live call.

Group Conversations

Create group chats for challenges, cohorts, or communities. Broadcast announcements to all clients or specific segments.

Auto-Messages

Schedule welcome messages, check-in reminders, and re-engagement nudges. Set it once, it sends automatically at the right moment.

Scheduled Messages

Write messages now, send them later. Batch your communication in one session and schedule delivery throughout the week.

Focus Mode

Set working hours. Outside those hours, clients see an auto-reply with your next available time. Protect your personal time without ghosting clients.
