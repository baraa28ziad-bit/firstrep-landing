Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI know what content to create?
When you set up your Growth profile, you tell us your specialty (strength training, weight loss, sports performance, etc.), your target audience, and your tone of voice. The AI uses this profile plus your existing content, reviews, and package descriptions to generate highly relevant posts, articles, and lead magnets that sound like you wrote them.
Do I need marketing experience?
Not at all. The entire system is designed for trainers who hate marketing. The AI handles content creation, SEO optimization, lead capture forms, and email nurture sequences. You just review what it creates and approve what you like. Think of it as having a marketing intern who works 24/7 and never asks for a raise.
Can I edit the AI-generated content?
Absolutely. Every piece of content — social posts, blog articles, lead magnets, and nurture emails — goes into a review queue where you can edit, approve, or skip. Most trainers find they only need to make minor tweaks before posting. The AI learns from your edits over time to better match your voice.
How many clients can I expect to get?
Results vary based on your niche, location, and consistency. Trainers who approve content weekly and have their mini-website active typically see 5–15 qualified leads per month within 90 days. The key is consistency — the AI handles the hard part, but you need to review and approve the content it creates.
Is the mini-website really free?
Yes. Every trainer on FirstRep gets a free mini-website at firstrep.fit/@yourhandle. It automatically showcases your packages, reviews, articles, and bio. There is nothing extra to pay, no hosting fees, and no domain to buy. It is included with both the Free and Pro plans.
