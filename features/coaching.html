Your Command Center for Client Success

Every tool you need to deliver consistent, high-quality coaching at scale.

Health Scores

Every client gets a dynamic health score based on workout compliance, check-in responses, nutrition adherence, and engagement. One number tells you everything.

Check-In Queue

Weekly check-ins organized by priority. AI drafts personalized responses based on client data. Review 20+ check-ins in 15 minutes.

Risk Detection

Automatic flags for missed workouts, declining adherence, low energy reports, and plateau signals. Catch at-risk clients before they churn.

Weekly Rhythm

Every week follows a structured coaching rhythm: check-in day, review day, update day. Your clients always know what to expect.

Client Assessments

Structured onboarding questionnaires capture goals, injuries, experience, equipment access, and preferences. Build the full picture before day one.

Automation Rules

Configure triggers and actions: missed workout sends a nudge, weight plateau flags for review, low compliance escalates. Your coaching runs 24/7.

An Escalation Ladder That Runs Itself

When a client misses a workout, the system responds automatically. Day 1: gentle reminder. Day 3: auto-message with encouragement. Day 5: trainer alert with full context. Day 7: flag for urgent review. You set the rules once — the system enforces them forever.
