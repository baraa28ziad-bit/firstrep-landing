Professional Tools for Professional Coaches

Streamline your admin work so you can focus on coaching.

Intake Forms

Build custom intake forms with text, number, select, multi-select, date, and textarea fields. Auto-send to new clients on signup. Review submissions in your dashboard.

Client Compliance

Track workout completion, check-in submission, nutrition adherence, and habit scores. Color-coded compliance bars show who's on track and who needs attention.

Discount Codes

Create percentage or fixed-amount discounts with usage limits and expiry dates. Track redemptions and revenue impact. Run promotions without third-party tools.

Form Builder

Drag-and-drop form builder with 6 field types. Add validation rules, required fields, and conditional logic. Professional forms without coding.

Client Flags

Flag clients for specific attention: missed payments, declining compliance, upcoming renewal, injury report. Custom flag types for your workflow.

Export & Reports

Export client data, revenue reports, and compliance summaries as CSV. Generate professional reports for client reviews and business planning.
