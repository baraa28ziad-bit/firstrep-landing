HABITS & GAMIFICATION

Turn Consistency Into a Game They Win

Daily habits, streak tracking, badges, challenges, and leaderboards. Keep clients engaged and accountable with built-in gamification that drives real results.

Download the App View All Features

Engagement That Drives Results

Make consistency fun. Your clients build habits that stick.

Daily Habits

Assign custom daily habits: drink water, take supplements, hit step goals, meditate. Clients check them off each day. You see compliance at a glance.

Streak Tracking

Every consecutive day builds a streak. Visual streak counters motivate clients to keep going. Break a streak? The system nudges them to restart.

Badges & Achievements

Auto-detected milestones unlock badges: first workout, 7-day streak, 100kg bench press, 30-day consistency. Celebrate wins automatically.

Challenges

Create time-bound challenges: 30-day abs, step competition, nutrition week. Clients compete on leaderboards and earn rewards.

Leaderboards

Rank clients by consistency, volume, or challenge progress. Friendly competition drives engagement without requiring your time.

Templates

Pre-built habit templates for common goals: fat loss, muscle gain, general wellness. Assign a template and your client's habits are set up instantly.
