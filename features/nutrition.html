Nutrition Coaching

Fuel Results With Smarter Nutrition

Auto-calculated macros, custom meal plans, food logging, and daily compliance tracking. Give every client a complete nutrition strategy without the manual overhead.

Start Free Trial View All Features

Complete Nutrition Management

Everything from macro calculations to grocery lists, built right into your coaching platform.

Auto-Calculated Macros

Set client goals -- fat loss, muscle gain, maintenance -- and the system calculates daily macros automatically. Adjust with one slider as needs change.

Custom Meal Plans

Build meal plans with drag-and-drop simplicity. Assign breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks. Auto-generate grocery lists for the week.

Food Logging

Clients log meals from a database of thousands of foods. Barcode scanning, recent foods, and custom entries. Daily macro rings show progress at a glance.

Recipe Library

Create and share recipes with full nutritional breakdowns. Clients browse your recipe collection and add meals to their daily log with one tap.

Daily Compliance

See which clients hit their macros and which ones missed. Daily compliance scores roll up into weekly and monthly trends for easy tracking.

Macro Adjustments

Adjust calories and macros on the fly. The system recalculates meal plans and sends clients updated targets automatically. No spreadsheets needed.

Meal Planning

Build Meal Plans in Minutes, Not Hours

Drag and drop meals into a weekly plan. Each meal shows calories, protein, carbs, and fats. Auto-generate a grocery list your clients can take to the store. Swap individual meals without recalculating -- the system handles the math.
