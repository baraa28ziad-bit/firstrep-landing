Background: My PT Hub

My PT Hub was founded in 2014 and has grown to over 130,000 users. It is a well-established platform that covers the basics: workout delivery, nutrition tracking, client management, and basic scheduling. It has a decade of stability and a loyal user base.

But the fitness coaching industry has changed dramatically since 2014. Trainers now need client acquisition tools, structured coaching systems, and burnout prevention — categories where My PT Hub falls short.

Pricing Comparison: FirstRep vs My PT Hub

Where My PT Hub Still Wins

  • Established track record: Founded in 2014 with 130,000+ users. A decade of stability, bug fixes, and refinement gives confidence in platform reliability.
  • Unlimited clients at $89.99/mo: For trainers with 75+ clients, My PT Hub's unlimited plan is cheaper than FirstRep's per-client pricing.
  • Comprehensive nutrition database: My PT Hub has a mature food database and nutrition tracking system that has been refined over many years.
  • White-label branding: My PT Hub offers custom branding options that let trainers present the platform under their own brand.
  • Desktop web experience: My PT Hub's web dashboard is well-suited for trainers who prefer working from a desktop computer rather than mobile.

The Bottom Line

My PT Hub is a reliable, established platform that handles the basics of workout delivery and client management well. But it was built for a different era. In 2026, trainers need client acquisition tools, structured coaching workflows, and burnout prevention — three categories where My PT Hub has significant gaps.

FirstRep addresses all three with an active marketplace, a structured coaching system, and a full burnout prevention suite. And it does so with a permanent free tier that My PT Hub does not offer.

My PT Hub manages your clients. FirstRep helps you find, coach, and keep them — without burning out.

🏆 Final Verdict

Choose My PT Hub if you have 75+ clients, want a stable platform with a decade of refinement, prefer desktop-first workflow, and do not need a marketplace or structured coaching system. Choose FirstRep if you want to find clients through a marketplace, coach more deeply with structured tools, prevent burnout as you scale, and start for free. For trainers building or growing their business in 2026, FirstRep is the more complete platform.
