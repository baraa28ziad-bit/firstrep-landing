FirstRep vs Everfit: The 2026 Feature & Pricing Comparison

February 27, 2026 11 min read
TL;DR

Everfit has solid workout programming tools but no built-in scheduling, a limited marketplace, and feature gating on higher tiers. FirstRep includes native scheduling with Google Calendar sync, an active client discovery marketplace, structured coaching (onboarding, check-in queues, risk scoring), and burnout prevention — all on every plan. Everfit is cheaper at 25 clients ($19.99 vs $44.50), but when you add the cost of separate scheduling tools and marketing spend to find clients, FirstRep's total cost of ownership is lower.

Pricing Comparison: FirstRep vs Everfit

Everfit uses client-count tiers: free for 5, $19.99/mo for 25, $49.99/mo for 100, $99.99/mo for unlimited. FirstRep is free for 3, then $19/mo + $1.50/client above 8. Everfit wins on raw per-client price at lower tiers — but does not include scheduling or marketplace, which are separate costs elsewhere.

Where Everfit Still Wins

  • Lower sticker price at 25+ clients: $19.99/mo for 25 clients is hard to beat on raw price alone. If scheduling and marketplace are not priorities, Everfit is cheaper.
  • Free tier for 5 clients: Everfit gives you 5 free clients vs FirstRep's 3. For trainers just starting with a handful of clients and no growth plans, Everfit's free tier is more generous.
  • Clean workout builder UI: Everfit's workout builder is well-regarded and intuitive, with solid drag-and-drop functionality.
  • Habit tracking: Everfit includes native habit tracking. Both platforms support this feature.

The Bottom Line

Everfit is a competent workout programming platform with a good free tier. But it is fundamentally a workout tool — not a business-building platform. No scheduling, a limited marketplace, and no structured coaching system mean you end up cobbling together separate tools for the critical parts of running a training business.

FirstRep gives you everything in one place: workout programming, scheduling, marketplace, structured coaching, and burnout prevention. The monthly sticker price is higher per-client, but the total cost of ownership — when you factor in scheduling tools, marketing spend, and time saved — is often lower.

Everfit helps you program workouts. FirstRep helps you run a coaching business.

🏆 Final Verdict

Choose Everfit if you only need a workout delivery tool, already have a full client roster, use a separate scheduling system, and want the lowest per-client price. Choose FirstRep if you want an all-in-one platform with scheduling, marketplace, structured coaching, and burnout prevention. For trainers building a real business, FirstRep eliminates the need for 3–4 separate tools.
